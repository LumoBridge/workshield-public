WorkShield is part of **VeyDigital**. It is a secure web-based documentation platform built for structured records, evidence preservation, controlled sharing, and review-ready workflows.

WorkShield is a documentation platform, not a law firm or legal service. It does not provide legal advice, legal representation, or attorney-client privilege. Optional AI assistance is informational only and does not provide legal conclusions or determinations.

---

## Overview

Through professional experience and research, I saw how difficult it can be for people to piece events back together once time has passed. Notes may be incomplete, messages may be scattered across different places, and important context can be easy to lose. In some situations, employees may also face conflicting accounts, incomplete records, or summaries that do not fully reflect what happened. Even when someone has a strong work history, that alone may not provide enough clarity if timelines, communications, supporting materials, and impact are not organized clearly.

I built WorkShield to give people a structured way to maintain their own records, preserve document integrity, and prepare clear, review-ready exports when needed.

## Structured documentation built for review readiness

Some features include optional **AI Assist** prompts to support structured intake and completeness. AI assistance is informational only and does not provide legal advice, legal conclusions, or determinations.

WorkShield is built around research-informed intake, evidence preservation, and export-ready organization for teams that need clarity, traceability, and strong record hygiene.

### Core capabilities

**📝 Incident Journal**  
**AI Assist (optional)**  
Structured intake for contemporaneous incident records with consistent fields across teams. You control what is captured and when.

**📎 Evidence Vault**  
Preserve attachments in an organized record linked to specific matters for easier retrieval. Sharing remains fully controlled by you.

**🔒 Integrity & Timestamps**  
Time-ordered records and integrity signals that support traceability and record hygiene. Built for preservation, not legal conclusions.

**📅 Timeline View**  
**AI Assist (optional)**  
A chronological view that helps surface gaps, duplicates, and missing details before review. Designed to support completeness checks.

**✓ Documentation Checklist**  
**AI Assist (optional)**  
Prompts that help teams capture important details with greater consistency and less guesswork. Informational guidance only.

**⏰ Timeline Alerts / Deadline Awareness**  
General awareness of documentation timing, chronology gaps, and common time-sensitive patterns. Not legal advice. Requirements vary by jurisdiction.

**📤 Export When Ready**  
**AI Assist (optional)**  
Generate review-ready exports for internal archiving or professional review. You decide what to include.

**💬 Support That Stays in Scope**  
Guidance on platform use and documentation workflows without legal advice or legal strategy.

## Additional platform areas

**Dashboard**  
A central view of incidents, evidence, timelines, and documentation status, making records easier to review, manage, and maintain over time.

**Resources**  
Educational and process-informed materials that support documentation practices, clearly labeled as informational and not legal advice.

**Managed Access for teams**  
Controlled sharing, without the clutter.

For teams of 2+, Managed Access adds stronger coordination, governance, and onboarding for shared documentation workflows.

- Shared workspace for coordinated documentation
- Explicit access decisions, with nothing assumed or automatic
- Export only when review or disclosure is appropriate

Managed Access is designed for teams that need structure around sensitive documentation without forcing automatic sharing. Teams can work in one shared workspace while individuals remain in control of what leaves it.
